View my Booklet PDF.

Image of Echo Park Lake lotus bloom in April 2015.

The PDF is a booklet for my final project in Journalism Design created on InDesign. The booklet is in collaboration with a project surrounding the idea of a Community Supported Textiles Day, inspired by a class I'm taking with Laura Sansone. The project aims to address the issue of waste in textiles and constructing means of access to textile resources for the people of Los Angeles, especially those in need of clothing. Essentially the project is for a community mending workshop and donation drive where we will construct mending toolkits in pocket-bags made from local beeswax and recycled textile scraps. The event will take place at Echo Park.

The imagery used in this booklet is credited, license free, and/or mine. Thank you for those who gave me permission to illustrate my booklet with their work.
