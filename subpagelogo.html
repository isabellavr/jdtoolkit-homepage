Below is a logo I have created on Adobe Illustrator for Journalism Design Toolkit Fall 2020 with Professor Jason Das.

Inspired by my summer at First Point, the logo's design is comprised of the color schemes of the beach. From the rows of multi-colored logs lined up against the Gidget wall, to the gradating green and brown seaweed piles polluted by synthetic pops of color, the logo's colors encompass my most recent revisiting of "home."

This fictitious logo is for either a record label or a record store.
